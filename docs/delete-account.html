ACCOUNT DELETION REQUEST

Delete Your Account

⚠️ THIS ACTION IS PERMANENT AND CANNOT BE UNDONE

You can request the deletion of your Nexus Athletics account and all associated data by following the steps below or by sending an email to our support team.

1
From the app: Go to Profile → Account Settings → scroll to the bottom → tap "Delete Account"
2
Confirm: You will be asked to confirm the deletion. Once confirmed, the process starts immediately.
3
By email: Send a request to support@nexusathletics.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and your registered email address.

DATA THAT WILL BE DELETED

Account & profile Workout history AI chat sessions Nutrition plans Body scan data Health & steps data Social posts & comments Payment history

DATA RETAINED (LEGAL OBLIGATION)

Transaction records — 5 years (tax law) Anonymized analytics — indefinitely
REQUEST DELETION BY EMAIL
Requests are processed within 30 days.
Active subscriptions must be cancelled before account deletion.
