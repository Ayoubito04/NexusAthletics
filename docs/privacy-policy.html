PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Nexus Athletics ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Nexus Athletics mobile application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile data: Age, weight, height, gender, and fitness goals you provide voluntarily.
  • Fitness data: Workout history, step counts, calories, and exercise sessions.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates during running or cycling activities (only when in use).
  • Photos and videos: Images you upload for body scanning or form analysis.
  • Audio: Voice recordings used for the Voice Coach feature (processed and not stored).
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe or PayPal. We do not store card details.
  • Device activity: Physical movement detected to recognize workout sessions.

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide and personalize the app experience
  • To generate AI-powered workout and nutrition plans
  • To track your fitness progress over time
  • To process subscription payments
  • To send push notifications about your training (with your consent)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Google Gemini AI: To generate workout and nutrition plans (anonymized prompts).
  • Stripe / PayPal: To process payments securely.
  • Cloudinary: To store profile photos and body scan images.
  • Supabase: As our database infrastructure provider.

All third-party providers are contractually required to protect your data.

4. DATA SECURITY

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We use JWT tokens for secure authentication.

5. DATA RETENTION

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 30 days, except transaction records which are kept for 5 years as required by law.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, visit: Account Deletion Page or contact us by email.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Nexus Athletics is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. LOCATION DATA

Location access is only requested during GPS activity tracking (running, cycling). Location data is used solely to map your route and calculate distance. You can deny location permission and still use all other app features.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
